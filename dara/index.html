    Drop phase

    Players alternate placing their tokens on the game board. Once all tokens have been placed, the move phase begins.

    Move phase

    Players take turns moving their tokens, one at a time, in an attempt to make 3 in a row. Once a player has made three in a row, he can remove one of his opponentâ€™s tokens from the board.

    Constraints

    • You may not have more that 3 tokens in a row.
    • You are not allowed to make 3 in a row during the drop phase.
    • You may not capture an opponents token when it is part of a 3 in row he made.
    • Rows of three may only be orthogonally, not diagonally.
    • No diagonal moves are allowed.
